
# HAIROTICMEN – Final Consumer Journey & Retail Execution Blueprint (Germany)
Date: 2025-10-04

## 0. Executive Summary
This blueprint defines the end-to-end online retail journey for the final consumer in Germany – from first touch to purchase to long-term loyalty – with administrative, operational, logistical, marketing, and advertising requirements. It is designed for implementation on hairoticmen.de and social platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube), with WooCommerce, myCred, DHL, TrackShip, NotificationX, MailPoet/Jetpack CRM, and a smart assistant (“Hairo Assistant”).

**Primary Goals**
- Launch confidently with a conversion-ready store and compliant legal base.
- Achieve sustainable CAC:LTV economics via loyalty & retention.
- Build brand preference using premium positioning and superior UX.

**Core KPIs**
- CTR ≥ 1.5% (prospecting), ROAS ≥ 2.0 (first 60 days), Conversion Rate ≥ 2.0%,
- AOV ≥ 35€ (starter), CAC payback ≤ 1 order for top products, Repeat rate ≥ 25% within 60–90 days.

## 6. E‑Commerce Operations (WooCommerce)
### 6.1 Catalog & PDP
- Attributes (Size/Scent), INCI block, usage steps, FAQs, cross‑sell mapping, badges (Vegan/ISO/GMP if applicable).
### 6.2 Payments
- WooCommerce Payments + PayPal + Klarna; antifraud checks; refund pipeline.
### 6.3 Shipping & Logistics (DHL)
- **Germany:** DHL Standard 2–3 Werktage; Free ≥ 49.99€ (retail). Berlin: Free/Local option.
- **EU:** DHL Zone 1, Free ≥ 99.99€.
- Tracking via TrackShip; proactive shipment emails.
### 6.4 Warehouse SOP (Pick & Pack)
1) Order import -> 2) Stock check -> 3) Batch picking -> 4) QC (seal/date) -> 5) Pack with filler & invoice -> 6) Label print -> 7) Handover.
- SLA: Orders before 14:00 -> ship same day (business days).
- Packing list in parcel; return label on request.
### 6.5 Inventory
- Reorder points per SKU (lead time + safety stock). Monthly cycle count.

## 8. Returns & Exchanges (Germany)
### 8.1 Policy
- Widerruf 14 Tage ab Zustellung. Ungeöffnet/Originalzustand für volle Erstattung.
- Geöffnete Kosmetik: nur bei Defekt/Qualitätsmangel (Fotos erforderlich).
- Kosten Rücksendung: Kunde, außer bei Fehler unsererseits.
### 8.2 Process
1) Online‑Formular -> 2) RMA & Label -> 3) Eingang/QC (48h) -> 4) Erstattung (bis 5 Werktage) oder Gutschein.
### 8.3 Templates (DE)
- Betreff: „Deine Rückgabeanfrage #{rma}“ – Inhalt: RMA, Label, Zeitplan, FAQ‑Link.

## 10. Risk & Escalation Playbooks
- **Shipping delays (DHL):** proactive email + 10% coupon + points goodwill.
- **Stockouts:** waitlist + restock email + alt recommendations.
- **Negative reviews:** response within 24h; resolution + loyalty gesture.
- **QC issue:** stop‑ship lot, root cause, replace/recall protocol.
